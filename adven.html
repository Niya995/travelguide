List of Adventurous Spots in Kerala

  • Parambikulam Tiger Reserve

    Located in the Palakkad district, this tiger reserve is a haven for wildlife enthusiasts. It's perfect for safaris, birdwatching, and exploring the rich biodiversity of Kerala's forests.

  • Athirappilly Waterfalls

    Known as the "Niagara of India," Athirappilly Waterfalls in Thrissur is one of the largest waterfalls in Kerala. It offers a thrilling experience for those who enjoy trekking and water-based adventures.

  • Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary

    This sanctuary in Wayanad district is a hotspot for adventurous activities like wildlife safaris, trekking, and camping in the midst of dense forests and pristine nature.

  • Varkala Beach

    Famous for its cliffs and scenic views, Varkala Beach offers a variety of water sports, such as surfing, parasailing, and jet-skiing, along with its picturesque surroundings.
