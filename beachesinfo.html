List of Beaches in Kerala

  • Varkala Beach

    Situated in Varkala town, Thiruvananthapuram district, this beach is renowned for its unique cliffs adjacent to the Arabian Sea.

  • Alappuzha Beach

    Located in Alappuzha district, often referred to as the "Venice of the East," this beach is known for its vast stretches of golden sand and ancient pier.

  • Muzhappilangad Beach

    Found in Kannur district, this is Asia's longest drive-in beach, stretching across 5.5 km, offering the unique experience of driving along the shoreline.

  • Bekal Beach

    Located in Kasaragod district, this beach is complemented by the historical Bekal Fort, offering a blend of relaxation and cultural exploration.

  • Cherai Beach

    Situated in the northern part of Vypin Island, Ernakulam district, approximately 25 km from Kochi city, Cherai Beach is ideal for swimming due to its gentle waves and is known for frequent dolphin sightings.

  • Kovalam Beach

    Located 16 km from Thiruvananthapuram city, this is one of Kerala's most famous beaches, known for its crescent-shaped coastline and lighthouse views.

  • Marari Beach

    Situated near Mararikulam village in Alappuzha district, Marari offers a serene environment with its pristine sands and coconut palm-lined shores.

  • Kappad Beach

    Located in Kozhikode district, this beach is historically significant as the landing spot of Vasco da Gama in 1498, offering a mix of history and natural beauty.

  • Payyambalam Beach

    Situated in Kannur town, Kannur district, this beach is known for its vast stretches of sand and is a popular picnic spot.

  • Meenkunnu Beach

    Located near Azhikode in Kannur district, Meenkunnu is less crowded and offers a peaceful retreat with its golden sands and clear waters.
